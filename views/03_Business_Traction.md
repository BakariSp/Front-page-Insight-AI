# Business Model & Traction

## 1. Business Model
- **B2B (School Side):**
    - Fee: 100k HKD / School / Year.
    - Includes: LMS, AI Grading, Data Analytics, Teacher Training.
- **B2C (Parent Side):**
    - Freemium Model.
    - Premium (680 HKD/Yr): Advanced Analysis.
    - Flagship (1280 HKD/Yr): AI Tutor + College Planning.
- **Economics:** ~70% Gross Margin; Target Renewal Rate >80%.
